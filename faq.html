Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the park hours?

Most parks are open daily from sunrise to sunset.

2. How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our online booking system on the website.

3. What activities are available in the parks?

We offer hiking, canoeing, fishing, camping, and more.

4. Are pets allowed in the parks?

Pets are allowed in some areas, but must be leashed at all times.

5. What should I bring for camping?

Pack essentials like a tent, sleeping bag, food, and cooking gear.

6. Is there an entrance fee?

Yes, there is an entrance fee, which varies by park.

7. Can I swim in the lakes?

Swimming is permitted in designated areas only.

8. Are there guided tours available?

Yes, we offer guided tours during various seasons.

9. What if I need to cancel my reservation?

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours prior to your arrival for a refund.

10. Are there restrooms available in the campsites?

Yes, most campsites have restroom facilities.

11. Can I bring my own firewood?

12. Is there a limit on the number of people per campsite?

Yes, each site has a maximum capacity for safety reasons.

13. What should I do in case of wildlife encounters?

Maintain distance and do not feed wildlife. Report aggressive animals to park staff.

14. Can I fish in the lakes?

Fishing is allowed but requires a valid fishing license.

15. Are campfires allowed?

Campfires are permitted in designated areas only and must be fully extinguished after use.
